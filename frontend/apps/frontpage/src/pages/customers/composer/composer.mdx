Composer, a cutting-edge investment platform, initially relied on a combination of tools from their broker-dealer to satisfy KYC. However, as they grew products (such as adding retirement accounts) and onboarded more users (7x growth in the last year), they recognized the need for a more robust and efficient KYC solution. Composer partnered with Footprint to rebuild their onboarding stack.

<Challenge>

- Speed and Efficiency: Manual approval process often led to significant delays, with customers sometimes waiting weeks to complete their onboarding due to manual reviews.
- Document Verification: Customers frequently had to submit documents via email, leading to inefficiencies.
- Need for Independent Security + Compliance: Composer was not just in the market for an onboarding tool. As they continued to grow, they recognized the need to own their own data and securely store that data. They were looking for both a KYC and Compliance tool as well as a data vaulting tool

</Challenge>

<Section>
### The Footprint Solution

**Accelerated processing times**: With Footprint, the average time to exit manual review dropped dramatically from six hours to just nine minutes. And less than 4.5% of applicants end up in manual review.

**Seamless KYC experience**: End-customers praised the ease of use and the secure document upload feature, enhancing their onboarding experience.

**Robust verification**: Footprint’s use of App Clips + ML Models for document verification dramatically reduced ID verification failures and lowered the need for constant manual checks.
</Section>

The integration of Footprint into Composer’s operations brought significant benefits:
