KYC (Know Your Customer) address verification is a critical process that helps businesses verify the identity of their customers and ensure compliance with anti-money laundering (AML) regulations.

It involves verifying a customer's physical address to confirm their identity and prevent identity theft, money laundering, and other financial crimes.

Inefficient manual processes

Manual address verification processes can be time-consuming, leading to delays in onboarding new customers and increased operational costs.

Automated address verification systems may generate false positives, flagging legitimate customers as suspicious.

Collecting and storing sensitive customer data, such as addresses, poses data security risks.
