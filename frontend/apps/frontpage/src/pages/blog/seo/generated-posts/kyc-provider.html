A Know Your Customer (KYC) provider is a company that offers services to help businesses verify the identity of their customers. This process is crucial in preventing financial crimes such as money laundering and terrorist financing.

What is KYC and Why is it Important?

KYC is a process that involves verifying a customer's identity to prevent financial crimes. It's essential for businesses to comply with anti-money laundering (AML) and counter-terrorism financing (CTF) regulations.
