What is KYC for Fintech and Why it Matters

Know Your Customer (KYC) is a process that financial institutions and businesses use to verify the identity of their customers or clients before cooperation and again periodically over time. Effective KYC involves knowing a customer's identity, their financial activities, and the risk they pose. KYC is required for any financial institution that deals with customers while opening and maintaining financial accounts. Read more about KYC for fintech and its importance.
