What is Fintech Identity Verification?

Fintech identity verification refers to the process of verifying an individual's or business's identity in the fintech space. This can involve a range of methods, including document verification, biometric authentication, and data analysis.

The goal of identity verification is to ensure that the individual or business is who they claim to be, reducing the risk of identity theft, fraud, and other financial crimes.
