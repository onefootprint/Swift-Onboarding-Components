Client onboarding is a critical process for any business, as it sets the tone for the entire client relationship. However, manual onboarding processes can be time-consuming, prone to errors, and frustrating for both clients and staff. That's where client onboarding software comes in – designed to streamline and automate the client intake process, making it more efficient, secure, and enjoyable for all parties involved.

What is Client Onboarding Software?

Client onboarding software is a type of technology that helps businesses automate and manage the client onboarding process. It typically includes features such as:

  • Electronic forms and signatures
  • Automated workflows and tasks
  • Secure document storage and sharing
  • Integration with existing CRM and project management tools
  • Customizable branding and templates
  • Identity verification and authentication, such as Footprint's triple binding identity approach, which verifies the person behind the screen, their device, and their phone number. This process is often used in conjunction with Know Your Customer (KYC) software to ensure a secure onboarding experience.

Benefits of Client Onboarding Software

Implementing client onboarding software can bring numerous benefits to your business, including:

  • Increased Efficiency: Automate manual tasks and reduce the time spent on onboarding clients.
  • Improved Accuracy: Reduce errors and inconsistencies in client data and documentation.
  • Enhanced Security: Protect sensitive client information with secure storage and sharing features, such as Footprint's seamless integration of onboarding with vaulting. This helps prevent account takeover fraud and ensures a secure onboarding experience.
  • Better Client Experience: Provide a seamless and intuitive onboarding experience for clients, leveraging native device experiences like Footprint's App Clips and Passkeys.
  • Increased Revenue: Get clients up and running faster, resulting in increased revenue and reduced churn.

How to Choose the Right Client Onboarding Software

When selecting client onboarding software, consider the following factors:

  • Ease of Use: Choose a software that is intuitive and easy to use for both clients and staff, like Footprint's user-friendly interface.
  • Customization: Select a software that allows for customization to fit your business's specific needs.
  • Integration: Ensure the software integrates with your existing tools and systems, such as Footprint's seamless integration with vaulting.
  • Security: Prioritize software with robust security features to protect sensitive client information, like Footprint's triple binding identity approach. This may also involve using identity verification tools to ensure the authenticity of clients.
  • Support: Look for software with reliable customer support and training resources.

Best Practices for Implementing Client Onboarding Software

To ensure a successful implementation, follow these best practices:

  • Define Your Onboarding Process: Map out your current onboarding process and identify areas for improvement.
  • Choose the Right Software: Select a software that meets your business's specific needs and requirements, such as Footprint's comprehensive platform.
  • Configure and Customize: Set up and customize the software to fit your business's workflow and branding.
