What is Customer Onboarding KYC?

Customer onboarding KYC is the process of verifying the identity of customers and assessing their risk level before allowing them to use a product or service. It involves collecting and verifying customer information, such as name, date of birth, address, and government-issued ID documents. Footprint's robust suite of tools and innovative approaches, including triple binding identity and device attestation frameworks, ensure accurate identity verification and prevent identity fraud.
