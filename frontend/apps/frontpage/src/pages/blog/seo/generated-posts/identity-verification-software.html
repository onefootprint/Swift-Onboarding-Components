What is Identity Verification Software?

Identity verification software utilizes additional personal information past a username or password to verify the identity of an individual.

This additional information comes in various forms, including date of birth, government-issued IDs, voice recognition, biometrics, or even facial recognition.

Footprint's platform takes this a step further with its triple binding identity approach, verifying the person behind the screen, their device, and their phone number for a robust and accurate identity verification process.
