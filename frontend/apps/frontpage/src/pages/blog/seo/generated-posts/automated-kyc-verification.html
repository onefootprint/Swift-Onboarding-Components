Why is KYC essential?

KYC is essential for businesses to comply with regulatory requirements and prevent identity fraud. It helps protect businesses from financial losses and reputational damage. This is especially important in preventing account takeover fraud. Industries that commonly use KYC include banking and finance, e-commerce, cryptocurrency, gaming, insurance, and real estate. Manual vs. automated KYC verification

Manual KYC verification involves human intervention to verify customers' identities and gather necessary information. Automated KYC verification uses advanced technology and software solutions to streamline the process, making it more efficient, accurate, and cost-effective.
