What is a KYC Form Template?

A KYC form template is a document used by businesses to collect information about a customer's identity, address, and other relevant details. The form is designed to help businesses verify the customer's identity and ensure compliance with regulations such as Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and Combating the Financing of Terrorism (CFT). How to Create a KYC Form Template

Creating a KYC form template is a straightforward process. Here are the steps:

Determine the Required Information

Determine the information required to verify the customer's identity and address.

Choose a Form Builder

Choose a form builder such as Jotform, Formplus, or Clustdoc to create your KYC form template.
