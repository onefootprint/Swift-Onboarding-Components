What is Digital ID?

Digital ID, also known as electronic identification or e-ID, refers to a digital representation of a person's identity. It's a unique identifier that can be used to verify an individual's identity online or offline, using a combination of biometric data, such as fingerprints or facial recognition, and other identifying information.
